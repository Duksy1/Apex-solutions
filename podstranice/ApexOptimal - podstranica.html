Frequently Asked Questions

Answers to the most common setup and compatibility questions.

Does it work with existing images?

Yes. Use the bulk action in Settings to optimize your full Media Library in one run.

Can I restore original images?

Yes. With backups enabled, each image can be restored directly from the Media Library.

Will uninstall break my images?

No. Optimized images stay valid and continue to work normally after uninstall.

Does it require Imagick?

No. ApexOptimal supports both Imagick and GD. Imagick gives better quality but GD is fully supported.

Does ApexOptimal send data to third parties?

No. Everything runs locally on your server, without external API calls or tracking scripts.
