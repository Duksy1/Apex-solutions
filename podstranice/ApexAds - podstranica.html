Every Ad Format You Need

From simple banners to immersive parallax creatives, popups, sticky bars, slide-ins and countdown timers — manage everything through a modern WordPress admin UI.

Upload images with target URLs, custom alignment, and CSS classes. Fully responsive with optional impression limits and device targeting.

Custom HTML Ads

3rd-Party Scripts

Video Embeds

YouTube, Vimeo, or custom video URLs displayed in secure, isolated frames for maximum safety and privacy.

Google AdSense

Enter your publisher ID and slot ID — the plugin generates the correct AdSense markup. IDs are automatically validated for security.

Parallax & Interscroller

Immersive ad formats with configurable scroll speed and viewport-triggered CTAs. Scripts load only on pages where these ads appear.

Popup / Modal

Modal overlay ads triggered by time delay, scroll percentage, or exit intent. Frequency cap: every visit, once per session, daily, or one-time only.

Sticky / Floating

Fixed-position ads that stay visible while scrolling. Choose from bottom bar, top bar, left or right corner. Cookie-based dismiss with configurable expiry.

Slide-in

Slides in from bottom-right or bottom-left corner when the user scrolls to a configurable percentage. Less intrusive than popups — ideal for CTAs and newsletter signups.

Countdown Timer

Countdown to a specific date/time. When expired: hide the ad or show a custom message. Configurable colors, content above timer. Perfect for sales campaigns.

Beyond Standard Banners

Parallax ads use adjustable scroll speed for subtle or dramatic effects. Interscroller ads stick between content sections and reveal CTA buttons as visitors scroll them into view.

  • Parallax: configurable height, speed, and background image
  • Interscroller: sticky creative with viewport-triggered CTA
  • Scripts loaded conditionally — only on pages with these ads
  • Lower speed values = more subtle, cinematic effect
  • Battery-efficient scroll detection for mobile devices

Popup Ads That Convert

Display modal overlay ads triggered by time delay, scroll depth, or exit intent. Fine-grained frequency capping ensures users aren't overwhelmed while maximizing conversions.

  • Triggers: time delay (seconds), scroll %, or exit intent
  • Frequency cap: every visit, once/session, once/day, once ever
  • Image banner + link and/or custom HTML content
  • Optional dark overlay behind the modal
  • Smart frequency memory ensures users see each popup only as often as you allow

Every click is verified, every export is protected, and every query is optimized. No shortcuts on security.
